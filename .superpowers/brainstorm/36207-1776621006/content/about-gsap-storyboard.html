“Soft reveal, sharper memory.”
The motion language is editorial, not flashy: photo and text arrive in a staged hero timeline, each following section reveals on scroll with staggered text/list movement, and the final CTA settles in with subtle depth. No pinning, no scroll hijacking, and reduced-motion users get static content.
